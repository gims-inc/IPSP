Inducing Factors for the Project Choice.

The development of this project was necessitated by a number of factors, which include but are not limited to:

  • Increasing demand for a particular service - If there is a growing demand for a particular service, it can be a good opportunity to develop an in-person service provider project to meet that demand.
  • Competitive market - The occurence of other service providers in the market, is one of the driving factors in this project This is to give us a way to stand out and differentiate from competitors, by enabling the clients access the service providers from a central point of administration.
  • Targeting a specific customer base - This in-person service provider project is developed to cater to a specific customer base, including demographic. A client is able to access a service provider regardless of the location.
  • Lack of quality service providers - There is a lack of quality service providers in particular areas, thus the developing of this in-person service provider project can be an opportunity to provide high-quality services and fill the gap in the market.
  • Personal passion or interest - Our passion for engineering and coming up with solutions to the real world problems was a key driving factor in this project. We needed to leverage on the new technologies and the automation of the current processes
